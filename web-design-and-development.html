Responsive Webdesign

Gone are the days where everyone used one of a handful of screen sizes. Nowadays, most of the professionals are using different devices at a time for surfing. To cope up with them, you need a Responsive website. It is not only about adjustable view of webpages, but it’s the other way of thinking about designing & has a great impact on your product / services. It is the approach that suggests that development & design should respond to users device, whether Laptop or Mobile device. It eradicate the use of different layout & design for every device available in the market.

It consist of flexible lattices and designs, graphics and a smart use of CSS media. All page components are sized by proportion, rather than pixels. As the user switches from their one device to another, the website automatically put up for resolution, image size and scripting abilities. In other words, the website should have the technology to automatically respond to the user’s preferences. We invest our precious time for our clients to amend changes on their click, as needed. This is why, we practice world renowned open source CMS, consenting clients to maintain their website with ease.

Custom Content Management System (CMS) Development

Wikipedia defines a Content Management System (CMS) as "a process or a system that is used to organize and facilitate collaborative collection of documents and other content." In short, a CMS is a web-based application that allows multiple users to manage different parts of a website at varying permission levels. With their permission levels, they'll be able to edit website content, data, and applications.
